About Me

Hi, I’m Nate Tibbals. I am a passionate, driven Software Developer with a strong customer centric background. I graduated from Eastern Washington University with a Bachelor’s degree in Communication with a minor in Business Administration. Since graduating, I have worked in several industries from telecommunications, to insurance, to online advertising. While these roles were primarily sales focused, they were all solution driven. This inspired me to shift away from purely customer facing, into being more hands on with technology and truly being a part of the magic that is creation. This is what set me on the path to become a Software Developer. I look forward to combining those two skillsets so I can have a positive impact on whatever culture and community I am part of.
